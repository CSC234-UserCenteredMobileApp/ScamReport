Fraud Alert
Surge in fake Songkran-themed parcel SMS scams
2026-04-23 • Posted by ScamReport Team
Over the past 72 hours, we have verified 142 reports of an SMS scam impersonating Kerry, Flash, and J&T Express. Messages typically read: "Your parcel could not be delivered. Confirm your address: [link]". The links lead to a near-identical clone of the real courier login page.

What to do:
• Never tap a link in a delivery SMS. Open the courier app or website directly.
• If you have already entered card details, contact your bank immediately to freeze the card.
• Forward suspicious SMS to 1185 (TruePalice) or report it here in the app.
